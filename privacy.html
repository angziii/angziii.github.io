Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how WHVer handles your information.

Effective date: April 13, 2026

1. Who we are

WHVer is an app designed to help users record, organize, review, and export working holiday visa related work records and supporting information.

2. Information you may provide in the app

You may choose to enter or store information such as:

  • work record details, including dates, employer information, hours, postcode, pay, payslip references, notes, and related evidence
  • optional account profile information, such as a nickname, avatar image, and Sign in with Apple account details
  • backup files that you export from or import into the app

3. How your data is stored

WHVer is designed to work primarily with on-device storage. Information you enter is generally stored locally on your device.

If you choose to enable cloud sync, certain app data may also be synced through Apple's iCloud / CloudKit services associated with your Apple account.

4. Sign in with Apple

If you choose to sign in with Apple, WHVer may receive information provided by Apple, such as your Apple user identifier, email address, and name, depending on what Apple makes available and what you choose to share.

This information is used only to support account-related features inside the app.

5. Photos and attachments

If you choose to use photo or image related features, WHVer may access images you select so they can be used as attachments or profile images in the app. The app does not access your photo library unless you choose to grant permission and select content.

6. Purchases and subscriptions

WHVer offers in-app purchases and subscriptions managed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card information. Billing, subscription management, and transaction processing are handled by Apple.

7. How we use information

We use information only to operate and support app features, including:

  • saving and displaying your records and app preferences
  • enabling optional account and profile features
  • supporting optional iCloud / CloudKit sync
  • enabling exports, backups, imports, widgets, and subscription access

8. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may be processed by Apple when you use Apple-provided services such as Sign in with Apple, iCloud / CloudKit, App Store purchases, or subscription management.

9. Data retention and deletion

You can delete data directly inside the app by clearing local data, removing account information, or deleting records individually.

If cloud sync is enabled, copies of your synced data may also remain in Apple's systems according to Apple's own retention and deletion processes.

10. Children's privacy

WHVer is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce privacy and security risks, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

12. Third-party services

WHVer may rely on Apple services, including but not limited to the App Store, StoreKit, Sign in with Apple, iCloud, and CloudKit. Your use of those services is also subject to Apple's terms and privacy practices.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the date at the top of this page.
