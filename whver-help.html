User Guide & Support

This page provides help and support information for the WHVer app.

Getting Started

  • Install WHVer from your mobile app marketplace.
  • Open the app and grant only the permissions you are comfortable with.
  • You can use the app without creating an account.

Common Issues

The app does not open or crashes

  • Force close the app and reopen it.
  • Restart your device.
  • Update WHVer to the latest version available in the app marketplace.
  • If needed, uninstall and reinstall the app.

Feature not working as expected

  • Make sure your device has a stable internet connection if the feature requires network access.
  • Check whether your system version is up to date.
  • Try again after restarting the app.

Privacy

WHVer does not require registration and does not collect personal information through normal use. For full details, please review the Privacy Policy page.
